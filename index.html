Help

Need help using NoteGenie? Take a look below!

Markup

To toggle markup view, press CTRL + M.

Headings

'#'s at the beginning of a line indicate headers. You can use up to 6 '#'s. The more '#'s, the smaller the header.

Quotes

'>' precede quotes
